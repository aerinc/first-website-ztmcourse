Easy Apricot Jam (Kayasi Reçeli)

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo of fresh apricots
  • 500 grams of white granulated sugar
  • Half of a lemon, squeezed juice

Directions:

Step 1: Wash all apricots and cut in half and remove the pits.
Step 2: Put all apricots inside a bowl.
Step 3: Cover apricots with the white sugar. Wait a minimum of 2-3 hours (overnight is preferred).
Step 4: In a metal pot, boil on low to medium heat for 30 minutes, removing any foam from the top.
Step 5: In the last 5 minutes, add the lemon juice and stir. Remove from heat and let chill. Store in a glass jar. Enjoy!

Easy Vegetable Omelette (Menemen)

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 large tomatoes, grated
  • 3 eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

Step 1: On medium heat, heat up the olive oil in a non-stick skillet.
Step 2: Add the diced onions and saute for 3 minutes
Step 3: Once onions are transluscent, add the bell peppers and sautè for another 3 minutes.
Step 4: Add the pureed tomatoes and cook for a few minutes. Add salt and pepper.
Step 5: Scamble the eggs in a bowl and add eggs to the onions, pepper and tomatoes mixture.
Step 6: Cook on low heat and stir with a wooden spoon. Bon appetit!
